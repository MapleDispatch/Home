Frequently Asked Questions

We gathered the answers to some popular questions below. If you can’t find your question below feel free to contact us, and we’ll be happy to help.

What dispatch services do you offer?

We offer dispatch services across Canada and the USA for Reefer, Dryvan, Flatbed, and Box Trucks, including 24/7 support, load tracking, billing, and administrative help.

Can you help new operators obtain necessary permits?

Yes, we assist with obtaining MC, DOT, UCR, and BOC-3 filings, guiding you through each step to ensure compliance.

What is your service fee?

Our fee is 5% of the gross rate for comprehensive dispatch services.

How can you assist in purchasing a truck and trailer?

We provide advice on purchasing 53ft trucks and trailers to maximize efficiency and profitability.

Do you recommend suppliers for equipment?

Yes, we offer a curated list of reputable suppliers for trucks and trailers to our clients.

How do you support with DOT inspections and audits?

We offer guidance and support to prepare for DOT inspections and audits, ensuring compliance.

Is there a discount available for dispatch services?

We offer a 16% discount on dispatch services for qualifying operators.

Do you have a driver list for hiring?

Yes, we maintain a list of qualified drivers for owner-operators looking to hire.

How can partnering with you improve my business’s profitability?

Our services reduce administrative burdens, provide access to better loads and rates, and offer compliance support, enhancing operational efficiency and profitability.
